Who we are

KOSENGINEERING LIMITED

Nigeria's Foremost Engineering Company.

Our Corporate Vision

To be the leading EPC Company Operating in multiple sectors in the Engineering and Construction Industry by focusing and providing our clients with responsible value engineered and innovating solutions to meet their needs for sustainable development.

Our Corporate Mission

Our mission is to operate responsibly as a professional with first class technology and to execute a sustainable engineering and construction model to the best satisfaction of our clients, be profitable & grow by capturing new opportunities

Our History

K.O.S Engineering Limited is an indigenous Engineering and Construction company, founded in 2019 and incorporated as a limited liability company in 2021.

With our well trained, certified, experienced personnel, we have the capacity and expertise needed to meet our client's every service requirement on time and on budget. From fabrication to scheduled maintenance to facility turnarounds to emergency response. We are committed to Safety, Quality, Delivery, Cost and Environment. We have implemented quality assurance procedures that are consistent with ISO-9001:2015 standards in preparedness for certification. We understand and remain highly committed to the quality and excellence demanded by industry-wide standards.

We Provide Quality Engineering, Innovative And Technological Service.
